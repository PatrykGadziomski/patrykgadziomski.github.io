About Me

I am a Data Scientist, Librarian and Manager, or Information Scientist for short. So if you need someone for Data Analysis, Machine Learning, Data Extraction, Knowledge Management, or data related Project Management, you've come to the right place!

More About Me

I am currently studying information science at the Stuttgart Media University, specialising in the data-related areas of my degree course. My primary interests include data extraction through web scraping, artificial intelligence, data analysis, and storage.

In addition to my studies, I also work as a tutor at the Stuttgart Media University and as a research assistant in the library for business administration at the University of Stuttgart.

I also have an interest in non-fiction literature and enjoy drinking tea and coffee. Additionally, I practice fencing during my free time.
